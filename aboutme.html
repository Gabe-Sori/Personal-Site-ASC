Hi my name is Gabriel Soriano and I was born in San Juan, Puerto Rico. My parents are from the Dominican Republic making me not just Puerto Rican but also Dominican. I was raised in Puerto Rico for 4 years which at the age of 4, I moved to America. That's when I had to start all over again: making new friends, learning a new language, getting used to the enviroment and exploring a whole new city. Because of this my mom took my brother and I to every borough. The
borough that I had the most fun in was Manhattan because that's where my mom took us to go ice skating (ice skating is one of my favorite hobbies).

I had always loved baseball baseball, whether it was watching, playing, or
practicing it. The issue about it was that I couldn't play it when I was
younger. The reason for this is because my parents were always busy with
work and never had the time to sign me up for a team. Since that was an
issue I decided to just practice alone at a park or some place where there
is a lot of space. Not being able to play in a team wasn't always the case.
Once I reached High School, I was finally given the option. I decided to
give baseball a try since I liked it my whole life and tryouts went pretty
good... The only issue was I was 251.4lbs. I could barely move fast enough
to make plays so I was benched the whole season. Sophomore Year I came
back with a new plan. I lost 20lbs. I was now able to move a bit quicker
and become a starter at 1st Base. Then I realized I want to win awards for
playing baseball, so my plan for this summer was to lose a lot of weight.
So I did! Junior Year I am 190lbs moving way faster than before and this
time I was able to play multiple positions. This year I was able to exceed
my goal by not only winning 1 but 2 awards. Making All-League
Selection: Secondary Team as Utility and Coaches Award.
